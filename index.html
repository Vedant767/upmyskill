FAQ

How do I calculate annual training package cost for my Company?
Training subscription cost is annual based upon total manpower working in the company.
Do I get Training Certificate?
Training Certificate is provided for only those Training which are under “Certification Training” category.

For other online Trainings if any organization want Training certificate, Soft copy of Training certificate will be provided at additional cost of 25Rs per certificate. Plz contact to Upmyskill for further information.
I have subscribed annual Training package for my organization, What happens next?
Our Training calendar is published on Upmyskill Website. You can plan at your end accordingly. 1-3 days Before Training date you will receive a Link to join the Training.

You can join through this link before start of the Training.
When do I have to pay?
Payment shall be done 100% advance for Yearly subscription. You will receive a Invoice from Upmyskill Team.
Do I need a specific software application to enroll on this course?
Zoom App is required to participate in Training
What languages is the course delivered in?
This course is delivered in English. Other Language can be used are Hindi, Marathi.
Why should we consider Web-based training?
Whether you are training a small group of employees at a single location or thousands of users spread worldwide, users have easy access to anywhere/anytime training, from home or work, day or night.

Web-based training can be just as effective as classroom training, and take half the time to complete at a fraction of the price.
What are the advantages of Web-based training?
One of the major advantages of Web-based training is the costs savings over traditional classroom training. Traditional training methods typically involve travel and time off from work, both costly components for employers
Can I customize Online courses?
Yes customized online Training can be planned for you at separate cost. This is not considered under annual Subscription Plan.
