FOUNDER

The founder at UpmySkill is Narayan R. Kavitake has completed B.E (Mechanical) From Govt. College of Pune (COEP) in 2001.

He is a qualified Lead Auditor for ISO 9001 & Internal Auditor for the automotive industry Standard IATF 16949, EMS-ISO14001 & Occupational Health & Safety-ISO45001 as well Laboratory Management System-NABL(ISO17025).

Prior to entering the training & Consultancy industry, he has initial experience in the automotive industry.

With over 22 years of hands-on experience in Production, quality management consulting, training & auditing in diverse organizations all over India since 2001, Narayan continues to provide Training & Consultancy for various sectors manufacturing companies.

With Aim to provide Trainings to all at affordable price Narayan has started UpmySkill which is Online Training Service solution with unique Annual Subscription Package.
